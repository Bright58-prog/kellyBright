About Me

Hello! I’m Abwa Zee Kelly Bright, a passionate and curious individual from Cameroon, currently pursuing a degree in Biochemistry. My academic journey is driven by a strong desire to build a foundation in science that will propel me toward a career in medicine, with the goal of becoming a cardiothoracic surgeon.

Alongside my studies, I have cultivated diverse skills in technology and creative arts. Through programs like Let’s Talk Tech, I gained hands-on experience in web development, video editing, song composition, and photography. Additionally, my six-months training at a computer workshop equipped me with valuable expertise in graphic design and various software applications.

Creativity and problem-solving are central to who I am. In my free time, I enjoy designing and building toy cars, a hobby that enhances my mechanical skills and innovative thinking. My work experience at a computer workshop has further sharpened my abilities in hardware maintenance and troubleshooting.

I am also passionate about entrepreneurship, actively exploring freelancing opportunities and the world of cryptocurrency trading to support my personal and professional growth.

Guided by faith, emotional maturity, and a commitment to lifelong learning, I strive to integrate technology and science to make meaningful contributions in healthcare. I am excited to share my journey and projects with you through this portfolio.
